Ribeye

Description

Salting the ribeye steak two days prior to cooking brines the steak, helps tenderize it, and adds rich flavor.

Info

  • Prep Time: 2 days
  • Cook Time:30 mins
  • Total Time: 2 days 30 mins
  • Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 6 (8 ounce) rib-eye steaks
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 Â½ teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, or as needed
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Meat thermometer

Instructions

  1. Brine the meat:

    Salt both sides of steaks with 1/2 teaspoon Diamond Crystal kosher salt per steak. Place steaks in an airtight container and refrigerate 48 hours.

  2. Prep for cooking:

    Remove steaks from the refrigerator about 30 minutes prior to cooking. Sprinkle both sides of steaks with black pepper.

  3. Prep the Pan:

    Heat a cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat until very hot. Add vegetable oil; heat until oil shimmers.

  4. Cook the Steak:

    Carefully place steaks in skillet. Add 1 tbsp butter per steak in pan. Continue flipping, browning both sides and allowing steak to reach desired doneness. Use a meat thermometer after 2 minutes and periodically thereafter in order to check the internal temperature. When Internal temp reaches 5 degrees F below desired doneness, remove and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.

    You may need to cook steaks in batches depending on the size of your cast-iron skillet.
